Chat with your home

You can chat with Voco an encrypted messaging network called Matrix. This is very similar to Whatsapp or Signal. To use it you'll have to intall a Matrix app on your phone, tablet of computer, and create a Matrix account.
Learn more

Matrix is like a cross between email and Whatsapp/Signal. Where Apps like Whatsapp have a central server that controls the entire chat network, with Matrix it's more like email, where lots of different servers exchange the messages. And as with email you can choose what provider you trust your messages with.

This design means no single organisation owns the chat network. Anyone can create a server, and then these servers route the messages between them so they get to the intented destination. You could even run your own server if you wanted, which would give you very high levels of control over your data.

This is made possible by the Matrix software that all these servers run. Matrix is open source, managed by the Matrix.org foundation, and supported by a worldwide community of people and organisations. The open source nature means the programming code can be viewed by anyone, including you. You can even help improve it. For many people this level of transparency allows for greater levels of trust.

The Matrix software can encrypt the messages, and Voco enables this for all the messages it sends.

You can learn much more at matrix.org.

Creating a Matrix account

Candle can create a Matrix account for you, on a French Matrix server called domainepublic.net. This means your messages will be stored in France. Candle has no relation with this foundation, and has no control over what happens on their server. But we do trust it.

How to use Voco

Voice control is really fun and useful, especially when you know your data stays in your own home. Here are some example commands. Voco is very flexible, you can say the commands in many different ways.

Turn things on and off

  • Turn on the livingroom light
  • Switch on the livingroom light
  • Switch off the livingroom light for five minutes
  • Turn on the livingroom light in 2 hours
  • Disable the livingroom light at a quarter past 4
  • Disable the security camera at lunchtime
  • Turn on the livingroom light from 4 pm until 5 pm
  • Turn off the livingroom light between 6 and 7
  • Open the front door
  • For 5 minutes lock the front door
  • Is the front door unlocked?
  • Is the livingroom light on or off?

Set things to certain values

  • Set the color of the livingroom light to red
  • Set the brightness of the livingroom light to 88
  • Set the livingroom light to 88
  • Set the color of the livingroom light to orange for 5 minutes
  • For 5 minutes set the brightness of the livingroom light to 88
  • Set the brightness of the livingroom light to 100 percent from 4 until 5 o' clock

Ask about certain values

  • What is the brightness of the livingroom light?
  • Can you tell me the color of the livingroom light?
  • What is the humidity level of the weather station?
  • What are the levels of the fine dust sensor?
  • What is the carbon dioxide value of the carbon sensor?

Set timers, alarms, reminders or a countdown

  • Set a timer for 5 minutes
  • Set a timer for 3 o' clock
  • At 9 pm remind me to go to the supermarket
  • Remind me to go to the supermarket at 2 o' clock tomorrow afternoon
  • In one hour and 15 minutes remind me to empty the washing machine
  • Set an alarm for 8 o' clock tomorrow morning
  • Set a countdown for midnight
  • How much longer does the countdown have to go?
  • Disable the countdown

Manage your timers

  • What are my reminders?
  • Tell me about the timers
  • Please give me a list of all my alarms
  • How much time does the countdown have to go?
  • How much longer does the countdown have to go?
  • Disable the countdown
  • Remove the last reminder
  • Remove all reminders
  • Delete all reminders
  • Could you please delete all the reminders?
  • Disable all the alarms

Other

  • Hello
  • What time is it?


Debugging is enabled

This addon is currently set to save detailed information about its operation in the internal logs. This may impact your privacy, as this also means that insights into your behaviour might be stored in those logs.

You can disable debugging mode by going to Settings, then Addons, and then clicking on the Settings button for the Voco addon. There you can uncheck the debugging option and click Apply.
